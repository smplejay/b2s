BUILT2SCALE Particles

This demo showcases an interactive background with animated particles that react to your mouse movement. The particles create subtle connections with each other and are gently attracted to your cursor as you move it across the screen.

Features

  • 40 floating particles with subtle appearance
  • Thin connecting lines between nearby particles (within 120px)
  • Mouse interaction with particles (within 100px)
  • Gentle attraction of particles to mouse cursor
  • Brand-consistent colors with Built2Scale orange accents
  • Thin line weights (0.5px) for elegant effect
  • Smooth 60fps animation with requestAnimationFrame

About This Effect

This type of interactive background adds subtle visual interest to a website without overwhelming the content. It creates a modern, engaging experience that responds to user interaction while maintaining the Built2Scale brand identity.

The particles are designed to complement the existing gradient background, adding depth and interactivity without compromising the site's performance or accessibility.
